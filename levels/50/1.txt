![](assets/start.svg) to create Carrot Instruction Set Code (CISC), that the

![](assets/play.svg) to try things out. Try things out :-)!

![](commands/forward.svg) to jump one field in the direction you are facing.

![](assets/reset.svg) and restart with ![](assets/play.svg).
